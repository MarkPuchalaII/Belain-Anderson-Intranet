Marketing Management

Marketing Management examines foundational marketing concepts.

Marketing is ever-present in our daily lives and this course will help students understand how organizations use marketing activities to create value for their customers.

Students will study the strategic marketing planning process and the marketing mix of product, price, place, and promotion.

Students will gain knowledge about the market research process and how data are used to inform marketing decisions.
