Change Management

Change Management provides an understanding of change and an overview of successfully managing change using various methods and tools.

Emphasizing change theories and various best practices, this course covers how to recognize and implement change using an array of other effective strategies, including those related to innovation and leadership.

Other topics include approaches to change, diagnosing and planning for change, implementing change, and sustaining change.
