English Composition I

English Composition I introduces candidates to the types of writing and thinking that are valued in college and beyond.

Candidates will practice writing in several genres with emphasis placed on writing and revising academic arguments.

Instruction and exercises in grammar, mechanics, research documentation, and style are paired with each module so that writers can practice these skills as necessary.

Composition I is a foundational course designed to help candidates prepare for success at the college level.

There are no prerequisites for English Composition I.
